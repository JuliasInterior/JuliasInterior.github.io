About

Sustainable Restaurant

Interior design concept build with only sustainable materisals.

The main idea of this Restaurant was to have little waste and use sustainable materials to make something that is timeless.The main material used is cork that is a material that can be reused and it has a beautiful sponge texture.

  • Client: Westminster Chapel
  • Category: Interior Design

Artist Residency

Conjoined resident spaces

This project was a concept of 2 residence spaces that had to be conjoined into one or connected by a bridge. The residence is a space for artist to spend their time relaxing but also working.Designed with 4 bedrooms and 4 studios, including private bathrooms each, the 3rd floor containing an open social area with an open kitchen, going to the 4th floor with a private gallery for artist to exhibit their work.The building had a number of spacious glass windows and on the roof used solar panels so it creates it own energy.

  • Client: Mahler And Lewitt Studios
  • Category: Architecture

Lamp Prototype

- Chocolate Blanc -

Chocolate Blanc is a lamp inspired by the look and taste of chocolate. Here Julia has created a lamp full of personality designed as a ‘sweet delight’ illuminating a dark space. Using a playful design process, Jula’s lighting design references a warm hue and geometric angles, luxury desserts and chocolate tastes.

  • Client: Claudia Florian
  • Category: Product Design

Chocolate Store

Chocolate Store Axonometric Sketch

My concept is about bringing people together through chocolate, the shop is designed to include all countries in the world. The chocolate is imported from different countries and they sell many different options, every week is different; space is designed in a way that is simple and works with all aspects of the countries every week as it changes, they can redesign the shop and make it represent the special day. The wall represents the rivers of the world and how they all go in the same direction.

  • Client: University
  • Category: Interior Design

Utopia Resort

Mezzanine concept

Utopia is a Project designed for the Barbican Conservatory where I designed a mezzanine for people to have a better experience of the space. Designed a captivating rail pattern the mezzanine includes a stage and a bar area where people can book for parties and for gatherings. In this area you can also observe there are allocated areas where people can spend their lunch or dinner enjoying a beautiful view of the conservatory.

  • Client: Barbican Conservatory
  • Category: Interior Design

Bedroom Design

3D Dream Bedroom Design.

A place like Home is a project about Julia's dream bedroom and everything she would include in it. A Bathroom, workspace, walking closet and even more.. She loves the idea of a balcony where she can go outside but not physically out of the bedroom so a balcony is a perfect space to take some fresh air just for a quick moment. The main concept of the room are the domes where you can watch above your head the sun ,rain, and the stars. It is an indoor outdoor experience and it reminds her of the days she used to go camping with her dad in the summer.

  • Client: Alex Ard
  • Category: Interior Design
